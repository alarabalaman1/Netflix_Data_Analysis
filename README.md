# Netflix_Data_Analysis
Data analysis project on Netflix content, focusing on content distribution across countries, ratings, years, and genres. The aim is to extract meaningful insights through data analysis and visualizations using Python.

## Figures

1. **Top 10 Countries with the Most Content on Netflix**  
   Visualization of the distribution of Netflix content across the top 10 countries, offering insight into regional content production.
2. **Number of Netflix Titles Released by Year (2000–2021)**  
   Line chart showing the yearly release trend of Netflix titles, illustrating the growth in content over time.
3. **Number of Netflix Titles Released by Year (2000–2021)**  
   Line chart showing the yearly release trend of Netflix titles, illustrating the growth in content over time.
4. **Top 15 Most Common Genres on Netflix**  
   A bar chart displaying the most frequent genres across all Netflix content, providing a snapshot of overall genre distribution.
5. **Top 5 Genres Over the Years**  
   Shows the yearly trend of the five most common genres on Netflix, highlighting how popular genres have evolved over time.


## Summary
- Content production increased sharply after 2012 but declined between 2018–2021. The decline is possibly due to Covid-19.  
- *Dramas* and *International Movies* are the most common genres overall, with over 4,000 titles each. 
- Content production is heavily concentrated in the USA, followed by India, UK, and Canada.
